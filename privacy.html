ClipDock Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-10

1. Who we are

ClipDock is a Windows desktop tool for organizing screenshots and managing a visual clipboard.

2. What data we collect

  • Images you capture/import: screenshots or images you paste/drag into the app.
  • Workspace metadata: necessary info to display/manage items (e.g., filename, size, grouping, timestamps).
  • OCR text (if enabled): text recognized from images for local search and copy.
  • Local diagnostic logs (optional): runtime logs used for troubleshooting (not used for advertising profiling).

3. Where data is stored

By default, all data is stored on your local device.

4. Cloud upload

  • No upload by default: ClipDock does not upload your screenshots or OCR text unless you explicitly initiate an action.
  • OneDrive migration (when you choose it): if you select a OneDrive folder and confirm migration, the app copies your local workspaces to that folder.
  • WebDAV backup (when you choose it): if you configure WebDAV and click upload/restore, the app uploads/downloads your workspaces backup file to/from your WebDAV server.

5. Permissions & system capabilities

  • Screen capture & window interaction: capture the screen and enable region selection.
  • Clipboard access: read/write images and text (e.g., “Copy OCR text”).
  • File system access: store workspaces locally, export images, migrate and backup data.

6. Credentials & sensitive information

If you use WebDAV, credentials are stored locally and encrypted on your device (e.g., using Windows data protection). We do not proactively collect or upload your credentials.

7. How to delete your data

  • You can delete images/workspaces within the app.
  • You can also delete the local workspaces folder to remove all data (path is shown in app settings).
